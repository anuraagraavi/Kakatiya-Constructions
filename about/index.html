Managing Partner's Message

Kakatiya Constructions is a leading construction company in Telangana founded in 1999. During 24 years of existence, we have grown from constructing small-scale projects to executing large-scale projects embodying our foundations.

Kakatiya Constructions has witnessed sustainable growth by prioritizing our fundamental principles, such as honesty and consistency. As a result, we have made considerable progress over the years, From executing small-scale projects to carrying out significant projects such as hospitals, educational institutions, and more.

This growth has been possible through our focus on investments in human resources, strategic planning, and teamwork. We are proud to maintain all the mandatory standards and regulations set by the Government of India, and we strive to keep the best of our standards. As a result, we delivered our projects with quality and effectiveness and completed them within the given timelines.

Chadalawada Sridhar
Managing Partner
